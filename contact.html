Common Questions

Do I need any experience?

Not at all! Our workshops are designed for all skill levels. We'll teach you everything you need to know.

What should I wear?

Comfortable clothes you don't mind getting a little paint or sawdust on. We provide aprons, but creativity can be messy!

Is food included?

Yes! Full-day workshops include lunch on the patio. Half-day workshops include snacks and beverages. Let us know about any dietary restrictions.

Can I book a private group?

Absolutely! We love hosting private workshops for groups of 4 or more. Contact us to discuss custom dates and projects.
