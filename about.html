Meet Judy

Welcome to Rolling Acres Studio! I'm Judy, and I've been creating art and teaching workshops for over 15 years. What started as a personal passion has grown into something I love sharing with others.

"There's something magical about working with your hands, creating something from nothing, and sharing that experience with others."

The Studio

Rolling Acres Studio sits on peaceful countryside acreage, surrounded by mature trees and natural beauty. The studio itself is a fun, welcoming space filled with tools, supplies, and inspiration.

Weather permitting, we extend our creative space outdoors‚Äîenjoying lunch on the patio, painting in the fresh air, or simply soaking in the tranquil setting between project sessions. During late spring and summer you can hand craft a bouquet of fresh flowers or book a photo shoot in the meadow.
